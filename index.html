An Estate of
Exceptional Character

Château La Chrysanelle is set within the prestigious Domaine Privé de Beauvallon in Grimaud — home to the oldest golf course on the Côte d'Azur. The Gulf of Saint-Tropez, its harbour, and the open sea unfold before you in a panorama that changes with every hour of light.

Conceived in close collaboration with a French architect, this château-style estate brings together refined proportions, Mediterranean light, and a rare sense of privacy. It is not simply a home. It is the expression of a singular vision.

A Singular
Vision

There are properties that are built, and properties that are created. Château La Chrysanelle belongs to the second category.

Every detail was considered with intention — refined, balanced, and brought closer to an ideal of timeless Riviera living. The result is a château-style estate of rare elegance in one of the most desirable settings near Saint-Tropez.

The central tower connecting the two levels is not merely functional — it is the architectural signature of the estate. The underground connection between the main residence and the guest house speaks to the same principle: beauty and intelligence working in unison.

To acquire Château La Chrysanelle is to acquire not simply a property, but a rare expression of Mediterranean elegance — a private estate shaped by architecture, light, and the art of living.
